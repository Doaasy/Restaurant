About

And then came ASTRA in 25th Dec 2003. It's never a copy of the franchise and pop up restaurants flooding Kuwait and claiming to be superior. It's much better than that, it's the purity of Kuwaiti minds working to deliver delicious bites from around the planet influenced by North Mediterranean cuisine and merging it with an immortal dynamic architecture and fluid divine music from all around the world. Making it a secret garden for the soul and a magical heaven for the illuminati. And if one thing we are proud of during all these years: it's how our beautiful loyal customers grow with us and refine us to enhance our product philosophies. Simply to say, amazing ASTRA was never created by us, actually when we think about it, amazing ASTRA was always mirroring YOU...
